About SkillExchange

SkillExchange is a platform designed to connect learners from around the world, enabling them to find study partners, join topic-based rooms, and share knowledge in a collaborative environment.

Our Mission

We believe that learning is more effective and enjoyable when done together. SkillExchange aims to foster a supportive community where users can:

  • Find and join study rooms based on their interests
  • Connect with like-minded learners
  • Share resources, ideas, and motivation
  • Grow their knowledge and skills collaboratively

Features

  • Create and join topic-based study rooms
  • Real-time messaging within rooms
  • Personalized user profiles
  • Activity feeds and room participation tracking
